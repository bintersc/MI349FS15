DM Tools

An Online Web App

A web application to smooth over the organization and ease the frustration of being a DM for a 3.5 edition campaign.

Dmdevtools305.herokuapp.com is a website intended for use in tandem with running a DnD campaign under the 3.5 edition ruleset. The core idea behind the site is that DMs who try for more realism almost always spend inordinate amounts of time searching through books of content, leafing through wikis online, or poring over character sheets. Dmdevtools305.herokuapp.com will allow DMs to create characters ahead of time and allow characters to interact while giving the DM relevant and organized data. This is not intended to replace the use of dice or as a standalone replacement to character sheets but a tool to make being a DM easier.
